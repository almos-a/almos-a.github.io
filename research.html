Research and Publications

MSc. Thesis Project: Development of an Autonomous Assistive Robotic System for Oral Intake Assistance of People with Disability

A vision sub-system based on the Kinect RGB-D camera for face recognition with 3D point cloud analysis to extract mouth feature points. Also, cup ROI detection by training a YOLO deep‑learning model and later performing image analysis to return the desired cup points and drink level. Co‑simulating the UR-10 6-DOF manipulator dynamics in MATLAB and ADAMS to perform analysis of the designed Hybrid impedance controller for a compliant assistive task execution. [Thesis Link]

A. Alwala, H. El-Hussieny, A. Mohamed, K. Iwasaki and Samy F. M. Assal, “Hybrid Impedance Control-Based Autonomous Robotic System for Natural-Like Drinking Assistance for Disabled Persons.”, International Journal of Control, Automation and Systems (IJCAS), 2022. [Paper]

Mobile Robots

Also, design of a mobile robot for specific person detection and tracking task by employing Open‑CV and deep‑learning approaches for edge devices like raspberry‑pi and jetson.
